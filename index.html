WRESTLEMATCH!

Memory Matching GameBy Gary Hughes

Choose Mode...

  • FATAL 4 (Easy)
  • KING OF THE RING (Medium)
  • BATTLE ROYAL (Hard)
  • ROYAL RUMBLE (Extreme)

How to Play...

  1. The aim: Match cards by their images until all cards have been revealed and matched up.
  2. You will receive a match rating as you play and a final match rating based on how many moves it takes you to match all the cards.
  3. To start a game, select 'START GAME' from the main menu.
  4. Select one of the four game modes, listed in order of difficulty from 'Easy' at the top to 'Extreme' at the bottom.
  5. You are now presented with a score panel and game board with a number of blank or unflipped cards.
  6. The score panel includes a match rating, number of attempted matches or moves, game duration time and restart button to reset the whole game.
  7. The game starts as soon as the first card selection is made and the clock starts ticking.
  8. To go back to the main menu, click or tap on the 'WRESTLEMATCH!' heading.
